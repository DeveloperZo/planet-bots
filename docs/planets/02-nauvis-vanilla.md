# Nauvis Vanilla

## Identity

The "real automation era." Full logistics and construction at scale. This is the upgrade moment the pre-vanilla phase builds toward. All planet families are situational specialisations relative to this baseline — not replacements.

The mod does not touch vanilla roboports, robots, or logistic chests. If pre-vanilla Field Drones crowd out the motivation to reach real robotics, the fix is tightening depot caps and charging throughput — not touching vanilla stats.

---

## Roboport — Vanilla Roboport (unmodified)

**Purpose:** Scalable construction and logistics networks across the entire base. The clear "you've made it" upgrade from Field Drones — removes the slot caps, radius limits, and throughput constraints of the pre-vanilla tier entirely.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Logistics radius | 25 (50×50 area) |
| Construction radius | 55 (110×110 area) |
| Charging energy | 1,000 kW |
| Charging stations | 4 |
| Robot slots | 50 |

---

## Logistic Bot — Vanilla Logistic Robot (unmodified)

**Purpose:** Automated item delivery and trash management. Unlocks the full logistic chest ecosystem — passive/active provider, storage, buffer, requester. Players stop hand-ferrying items.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
